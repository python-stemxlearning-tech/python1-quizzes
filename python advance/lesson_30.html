🔥 A junior analyst has inventory.csv with columns — Product, Category, Unit_Price, Stock, Reorder_Level. He runs this code. What will be the final output and what will be saved in reorder_list.csv? 
df = pd.read_csv("inventory.csv")
df = df.drop_duplicates()

Q1 = df["Unit_Price"].quantile(0.25)
Q3 = df["Unit_Price"].quantile(0.75)
IQR = Q3 - Q1
df = df[(df["Unit_Price"] >= Q1 - 1.5 * IQR) & 
        (df["Unit_Price"] <= Q3 + 1.5 * IQR)]

reorder = df[df["Stock"] < df["Reorder_Level"]]
reorder.to_csv("reorder_list.csv", index=False)
print(len(reorder))
